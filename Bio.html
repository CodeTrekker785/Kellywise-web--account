Who is DJ KELLYWISE?

Born and raised in the vibrant heart of Port Harcourt, Nigeria, DJ KELLYWISE (The Wise One) has redefined what it means to be a modern-day turntablist. With a career spanning over a decade, he has evolved from a local talent into a global sonic force.

Known for his "seamless fusion" style, Kellywise blends Afrobeats, Amapiano, Hip-Hop, and House music with a precision that keeps dancefloors electric from dusk till dawn. His philosophy is simple: "Music is a universal language, and I'm just the translator."
